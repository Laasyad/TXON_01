Coca-Cola, or Coke, is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by the Coca-Cola Company.
It was invented in the late 19th century by John Stith Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia.
Based on Interbrand's "best global brand" study of 2020, Coca-Cola was the world's sixth most valuable brand.
